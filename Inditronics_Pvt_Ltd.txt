🔧 How It Works:
Sensors/Devices collect data (e.g., temperature, motion).

Data is sent to the Internet using Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or mobile networks.

Cloud or Edge Computing processes the data.

Data is used to automate actions or sent to an app to monitor or control.
